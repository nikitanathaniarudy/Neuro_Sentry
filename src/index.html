How It Works

1. Start Scan

The user initiates a scan from our web-based kiosk. A series of simple prompts begins.

2. Data Capture

A standard webcam records video, capturing facial geometry, skin perfusion for vitals, and audio for speech tasks.

3. AI Analysis

Our backend engines process the video and audio streams in real-time, extracting key neurological indicators.

4. Get Results

A comprehensive dashboard displays a risk score, triage level, and detailed rationale in seconds.

About Neuro Sentry

Neuro Sentry is a proof-of-concept project designed for hackathon demonstration. It showcases a "live-only, no storage" architecture, meaning all data is processed in-memory for a single session without being saved. This ensures complete user privacy while demonstrating the power of real-time, AI-driven medical triage. The system is designed for rapid deployment in settings like emergency rooms, telehealth consultations, or remote clinics to assist healthcare professionals in making faster, more informed decisions.
